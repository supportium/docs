# General FAQ | Supportium AI Docs

## General

### Will Supportium AI work offline?

No, Supportium AI requires an internet connection to function as it needs to process queries through our AI infrastructure.

### Does Supportium AI have internet access?

No, Supportium AI does not have general internet access. This ensures that all responses are based on your provided knowledge base, maintaining accuracy and preventing hallucinations.
